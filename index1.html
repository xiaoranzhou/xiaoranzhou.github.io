About me

Xiaoran Zhou is a data science researcher at Forschungszentrum Jülich. He holds a Master's and Bachelor's degree in viticulture and enology. His doctoral research focused on developing models to study how carbon and water move through plants as they grow. He also researches data management in plant sciences.
